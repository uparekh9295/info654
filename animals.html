Meet our happy farm animals

Each one has a job - some give wool or eggs, some guard, and some just love to be cuddled.

🦙 Alpacas

The Marshmallows

Gentle and calm animals. Their fleece becomes soft scarves and hats.

  • Food: grass and hay
  • Fun fact: they hum when happy!

Wool Use

We shear them in spring and spin the wool locally.

🐑 Sheep

Heritage Flock

They help keep the orchard grass short and tidy.

Lambing Time

Happens late winter to early spring. Visits are quiet and gentle.

🐕 Farm Dogs

Guardian Angels

They protect the animals and welcome visitors.

Visitor Tips

Please ask before petting. No outside treats.

🦢 Geese

Honking Patrol

They act as our alarm and lawn mowers too!

Feather Crafts

We collect fallen feathers for small crafts.

🐔 Chickens

Colorful Flock

Our hens lay different colored eggs - brown, blue, and green.

Egg Care

Eggs are collected twice a day and kept cool.
