Tree Revival Program

From storm-battered trees to new life - our story of care, patience and teamwork.

Our Story

After a devastating hurricane, our century-old Mango, Falsa, and Eucalyptus trees were left uprooted and broken. For days, the entire community - neighbors, farmers, friends - came together with trucks, ropes, and hands full of hope We lifted these fallen giants carefully, restored their roots, and began a slow journey of revival.

Over the following years, with simple care and dedication, new leaves appeared, then flowers, and finally fruit. Today, these same trees stand tall again - a living reminder that with love, teamwork, and the right care, nature can heal itself.

Our Techniques

🌾 Farming - Gentle, Natural Care

  • Use dry leaves or straw around trees to keep soil cool.
  • Make small basins for slow watering.
  • Feed soil with compost, not chemicals.
  • Remove weeds by hand and let soil breathe.

🌱 Afforestation - Planting & Protecting New Life

  • Pick local tree types that belong here.
  • Mix soil with compost before planting.
  • Support saplings with simple sticks or ropes.
  • Protect young plants with guards from animals.
  • Water regularly for the first two years.

🌳 Tree Revival - Bringing Back the Old

  • Fix soil near roots after storms.
  • Cut broken branches carefully.
  • Keep water balanced — not too much or too little.
  • Use compost and keep checking growth.
  • Be patient — trees take time to heal.
